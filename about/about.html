Information about us


Dentistry, one of the oldest areas of medicine, is developing very dynamically today: new equipment, new materials, new methods of prevention, prosthetics and complex dental treatment appear every year.

Our dental clinics are equipped with first-class diagnostic and treatment equipment; the most modern dental materials are used in the process of complex treatment of neglected teeth.

The true wealth in our clinic is dentists of the highest category, their knowledge, ability to understand, feel the problems of the patient, the ability to help him.

Most dentists, orthopedists, surgeons, orthodontists, periodontists and implantologists of the highest category have significant clinical experience behind them. However, in the end, they chose our clinic due to well-organized working conditions, high-quality equipment, understanding of their aspirations and requirements by the management.
